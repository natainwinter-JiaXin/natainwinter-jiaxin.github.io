I wake up. I stay in bed an hour longer than i’m supposed to. I drink my green tea with honey and procrastinate adult life by reading a French book, I barely speak French. I push my body into positions that’ll give me backache in five years’ time, but at least i’m comfortable now. I forget to wish my mom a happy birthday because I am a bad daughter. I ignore all the messages I receive on dating apps, but I will later complain how nobody is falling in love with me when I’ve put so much work into making myself loveable. I make another green tea and I think, god, this is actually my life.
And now winter is coming, I will feel more fragile than ever
